Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?

We deliver your complete website in just 3 days (72 hours) from the time you provide all necessary content and materials.

What do I need to provide?

We'll need your business information, logo (if you have one), photos of your work, service descriptions, and any specific preferences for colors or design style.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes! We include a simple content management system that allows you to update text, images, and prices without any technical knowledge.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes, all our packages include secure hosting with SSL certificate for the first year. After that, hosting is available for a small annual fee.

What if I need changes after launch?

We offer ongoing support and can make updates as needed. Minor text changes are often free, while larger redesigns are quoted separately.
